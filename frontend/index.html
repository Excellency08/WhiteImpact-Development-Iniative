About WhiteImpact Development Iniative

Empowering Underserved Youth Through Inclusive Education, Digital Skills & Gender Justice

WhiteImpact Development Iniative was founded in 2025 and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria in 2025, in response to the intersecting humanitarian, social, and governance challenges affecting underserved communities across Nigeria.

As a youth-led Non-Governmental Organisation, we believe communities experiencing crisis are not passive beneficiaries of aid, they are the active leaders of change. Our work strengthens access to education, protection services, safe civic spaces, and digital opportunity for young women, persons with disabilities, and displaced populations.

What guides us

Our Core Values

Principles that shape every program, partnership, and decision we make.

Inclusion

We ensure underserved groups—especially young women and persons with disabilities—are actively involved in our programs and decision-making.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and ethical responsibility, building trust with communities, partners, and stakeholders.

Innovation

We embrace creative, forward-thinking approaches—leveraging technology and new ideas to solve complex social challenges.

Collaboration

We work with communities, partners, and institutions to strengthen impact through shared goals and collective action.

Sustainability

We design programs that create long-lasting change, building resilience and self-reliance in the communities we serve.

Equity

We are committed to fairness in access, opportunity, and outcomes—ensuring no one is left behind because of background, gender, or ability.
